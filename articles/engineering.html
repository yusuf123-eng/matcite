What is Engineering

Engineering is a branch of science and technology that uses scientific principles to design and build machines, engines, structures, and other items.

Types of Engineering

  • Aerospace engineer
  • Mechanical engineer
  • Biomedical engineer
  • Electrical engineer
  • Chemical engineer
  • Computer engineer
  • Civil engineer
  • Petroleum engineer

What does an engineer do

  • They design machines, build skyscrapers, and oversee public works
  • They design machinery
  • They use science, technology, and math to solve problems
  • They develop and build machines
  • Engineers are problem solvers
  • Engineers perform tasks like designing infrastructure, manufacturing personal hygiene products, or developing railways

Quality of a good engineer

  • Quantitative skills
  • Logical Thinking and Reasoning
  • Communication Skills
  • Attention to Detail
  • Creativity and Innovation
  • Team Player
  • Creative problem-solving
  • Mad Math Skills
  • Common sense
  • Natural Curiosity
  • Problem Solving Skills
  • Strong time management skills
  • Mechanical aptitude
  • A strong desire for organization and efficiency
  • Excellent communication/salesmanship
  • Math skills
