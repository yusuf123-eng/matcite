Matcite

Established Since 2020 by Yusuf Usman Dauda, togther with his co-founders; Usman Dauda, Haidar Sabo, Muhammad Usman and Sadiq Sabo. Created with the goal of providing online learning resources that help educate students.

The organization produces short lessons in the form of videos. Its website also includes tutorials and articles for educators. All resources are available for free to users of the website and app.

Our mission is to bring affordable education to people everywhere, in order to help them achieve their dreams and change the world.

Matcite is an organization that offers free educative tutorials, articles, courses for people all over the world.

Matcite

Matcite is an organization that offers instructional videos and articles that empower learners to study at their own pace in and outside of the classroom. We tackle math, science, computing, history, art history, economics, and more.
